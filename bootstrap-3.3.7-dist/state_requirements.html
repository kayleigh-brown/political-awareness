Registering to Vote

Registering to vote is an important process, because without a voter's registration you won't be able to vote in most states. There are several ways to get a voter's registration. Let's walk through each of them.

Voting Online
Voting online is available for 31 states and the District of Columbia. Register to vote here.

Voting In-Person
To vote in person, download the National Mail Voter Registration Form. Once this form is completed, remember to sign it before mailing it to the desired location for your state. You may also be able to register at your local election office, DMV, or military recruitment center.

Voting in Another Language
Fill out the National Mail Voter Registration Form, available in other languages.
Voter's guides are also very helpful resources.
