Donald Trump


Political Overview
 • Contender for the nomination of the Reform Party in 1999 who won the presidential primary in California
 • Featured speaker at the Conservative Political Action Confrence (CPAC)

Early Life and Education
 • Born 14 June 1946 in Queens, New York City, New York
 • Attended Fordham University after spending high school in the New York Military Academy
 • Graduated from the University of Pennsylvania's Wharton School of Finance

After College
 • Has a currently estimated net worth of $4.5 billion
 • Former reality TV star
 • Had a hit TV show, The Apprentice, on NBC, which was turned into The Celebrity Apprentice
 • Designed the Grand Hyatt in Manhattan at the age of 34
 • Later renovated the Plaza Hotel and the Barbizon, both located next to Central Park
 • President of the Trump Organization
 • Developed the Trump Tower in New York
 • Owned part of the Miss Universe, Miss USA, and Miss Teen USA beauty pageants
 • Founder of Trump University
 • Two time nominee for an Emmy award
