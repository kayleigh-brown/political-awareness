Gary Johnson


Political Overview
 • Governor of New Mexico for two terms
 • Vetoed 200 of the 424 bills he was presented during his first 6 months as governor
 • Ran for president in 2011

Early Life and Education
 • Born 1 January 1953 in Minot, North Dakota
 • Graduated with a B.S. in political science from the University of New Mexico

After College
