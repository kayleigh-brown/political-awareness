Darrell Castle


Political Overview
 • Two terms as the Constitution Party of Tennessee Chairman
 • Three terms as the Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Constitution Party
 • Two terms as the Platform Chairman of the National Convention of the Constitution Party
 • Board member of the conservative caucus
 • Former Chairman of the National Veterans Coalition
 • Former Constitution Party VP running mate

Early Life and Education
 • Born 1948 in Kingsport, Tennessee
 • Graduated from East Tennessee State University with a B.S. in both Political Science and in History
 • Earned his Juris Doctor from the University of Memphis

After College
 • Served in Vietnam
 • Served for four years in the U.S. Marine Corps, earning the rank of first lieutenant

Economic Stances
 • The Federal Reserve must be ended by repealing the Federal Reserve Act
 • Lenders and borrowers should be able to set their own interest rates
 • The American people should be free to use whatever currency they wish
 • The dollar will once again be exchangable for gold
 • Abortion must be ended, with no excpetions
 • The federal government will not spend any money funding Planned Parenthood or other abortion providers
 • The Supreme Court must not have jurisdiction over such matters as funding abortion

Foreign Stances
 • The U.S will seceed from the United Nations and NATO

Government Reform
 • The government must once more adhere to the Constitution strictly as it was written and intended
 • Agenda 21: The federal government must be withdrawn from international, sovereignty-destoring legislation

Scandals
